Our Story

Hi readers! We are the Turtle Heroes. The organisation started as a group of friends who have a passion for saving turtles which has now developed into a international charity foundation dedicated to saving Turtles over the ocean. Our staff and volunteers from all over the world have worked fantastically to save the turtles, promote turtle awarenesss and provide educational workshops to the public, schools and organisations to teach the marine environment and saving stranded turtles. We have many opportunities for you to join us or donate money to the foundation. We hope you enjoy our new website, and please leave us a message! We would love to hear from you.
