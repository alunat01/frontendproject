History of Turtles

Turtles are belived to have existed in the late Triassic Period of the Mesozoic era, around 215-220 million years ago. Turtles are known to be one of the oldest creatures on Earth before dinosaurs with the most ancient fossils found in Germany.

Did you know?

  • Turtles can live in almost any climate like underwater and on land
  • Sea Turtle mainly live in the ocean, only visiting the nearby beaches to lay eggs
  • The smalllest turtle has been recorded to weigh around 142 grams and one of the largest turtle species weighs around 680 grams
  • Some turtles are vegetarians, eating only grass and algae, whereas other turtles are omnivores eating fishes in the ocean
  • Although turtles may travel with other turtles, they do not interact or socialise with them
  • Turtles can live upto approximately 80 years with some of the larger turtles living for around 200 years!
